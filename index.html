What is OTC Derivatives?

Over-the-counter (OTC) derivatives are financial contracts not traded on an exchange but negotiated directly between parties.

Types of OTC Derivatives

Includes a variety of instruments such as swaps, forwards, and options, each with unique characteristics and purposes.
